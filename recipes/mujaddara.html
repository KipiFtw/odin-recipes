Mujaddara

The best lentil and rice meal from the middle east

Great and healthy meal, and its totally vegan!

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Lots of onion
  • Brown lentils
  • Rice
  1. Fry onion slices in olive oil. add salt to draw their water out. this will take a long time.
  2. Soak lentils in water for 20 minutes, then dry them
  3. Cook rice normally, adding some extra water for lentils. throw the lentils in to the pot when you add the water.
  4. When the rice and lentils are done, mix in the fried onion.

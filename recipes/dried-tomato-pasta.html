Cocoa Brownies

Dried cherry tomatos andother goodies on pasta, a real treat

Simple pasta recipe for when you are looking to impress

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Tomatoes
  • Red Onion
  • Garlic
  • Mushrooms (optional)
  • Basil
  • Salt
  • Parmesan
  1. Halve tomatoes, onions, garlic and mushrooms if you are using them. Quarter onion halves.
  2. Drizzle vegetables with olive oil and bake at 160 c for 40-60 minutes until desired done-ness
  3. While veggies are baking, cook pasta normally, keep some of the pasta water
  4. grate parmesan cheese on top of pasta, add olive oil and some pasta water and mix
  5. when vegetables are done, add them to pasta and mix
